# Spiceworks Help Desk Setup and Ticket Workflow

## Purpose

This lab focuses on understanding help desk ticket workflows using Spiceworks.

---

## Tasks Performed

* Installed and configured Spiceworks Help Desk
* Explored ticket creation and management
* Practiced ticket categorization and prioritization
* Simulated basic support ticket workflows

---

## Example Ticket Scenarios

* Outlook not syncing
* Slow computer performance
* Wi-Fi connectivity issues
* Printer offline issue

---

## Skills Practiced

* Ticket management
* Help desk workflows
* Issue categorization
* Technical documentation

---

## Outcome

Improved understanding of IT support ticket handling and service desk workflows.
